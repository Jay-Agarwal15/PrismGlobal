About

We started because quoting a part took too long.

Prism Global was founded by manufacturing engineers who were tired of waiting a week to find out whether a design was even feasible. Everything we've built since has been in service of closing that gap.

Our story

From one factory floor to a global network

We opened our first machine shop in 2011 with a handful of CNC mills and a simple idea: if a quote can be calculated, it shouldn't take a person three days to calculate it. That idea became a platform, and the platform let us extend beyond our own factory floor into a vetted network of partner facilities — so the capability list grew without the quality control loosening.

Today we run our own machining and assembly operations, work alongside hundreds of qualified partner facilities for molding and fabrication at scale, and ship finished parts to engineering teams on every populated continent.

2011

First machine shop opens, focused on CNC prototyping for local product teams.

2015

Added injection molding and sheet metal fabrication; began building the quoting engine in-house.

2018

Launched the online platform with instant quoting and the first version of automated DFM checks.

2022

Crossed 700 partner facilities and opened regional logistics hubs to cut shipping times.

2026

Expanded NPI services and rolled out richer production tracking with inspection photo capture.
