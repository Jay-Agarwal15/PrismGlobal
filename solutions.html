Built for wherever your part is in its life.

A first prototype and a 50,000-unit production order need different things from a manufacturing partner. Prism Global is built to carry a part through every stage without forcing a vendor change in between.

Rapid prototyping

Validate a design in days, not weeks

Get functional prototypes fast with CNC, 3D printing, or vacuum casting — whichever process matches the tolerance and material you actually need to test. DFM feedback on upload means you find design issues before you pay for a second iteration.

Low-volume production

Produce hundreds without committing to thousands

No minimum order quantity on molding or machining means you can serve a launch, a pilot run, or a niche product line without overbuilding inventory or overpaying for tooling you'll only use once.

Bridge to scale

Keep shipping while production tooling catches up

When demand outpaces your primary line, bridge production keeps parts moving using existing tooling and our partner network — so a supply gap doesn't become a stockout.

New product introduction

One partner from concept sketch to first shipment

Our NPI teams sit between design and production, catching the handoff issues that usually appear only after tooling is cut: DFM review, cost-down planning, and a manufacturing plan built around your launch date.
